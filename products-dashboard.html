5 Key Analysis Questions & Answers

Q1 — What is the product distribution across categories?
The top categories are Automotive, Health & Wellness, and Cleaning Supplies, each with 6 products. The catalog spans 32 distinct categories, with most having 2–3 products, indicating a wide but shallow inventory.
Q2 — Which categories command the highest average prices?
Clothing & Apparel ($858) tops the list, followed by Cycling ($799) and Home Decor ($782.50). Surprisingly, Smartwatches average only $32, showing large price disparity across tech categories.
Q3 — What is the current stock availability status?
Only 20% of products are immediately available (in_stock). The majority are on pre-order (25%) or backorder (16%), while 10 products are out of stock and 12 have been discontinued — a concerning supply chain signal.
Q4 — Which products have the highest inventory levels?
The Eco Heater Toaster Stove (Cleaning Supplies) leads with 998 units, followed by the Keyboard Toaster Monitor (988 units). Notably, the top 5 stocked products span very different categories.
Q5 — Is there a relationship between stock levels and availability status?
Interestingly, discontinued products hold the highest average stock (625 units) — suggesting items were removed from sale despite supply. In-stock items average 570 units; pre-order items have the lowest average at 519 units.
